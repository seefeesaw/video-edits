Section 05

Guardrails: a layered defense

No single safeguard is enough. Layered, overlapping checks catch what any one alone would miss.

Relevance classifierFlags off-topic queries before they reach the agent
Safety classifierDetects prompt injection and manipulation attempts
PII filterPrevents exposure of personally identifiable information
ModerationFlags harmful or inappropriate content in either direction
Tool safeguardsRates tool calls by risk and gates the dangerous ones
