Initialize dp of size total plus 1 with total plus 1, then set dp zero to 0. Outer loop: each amount from 1 to total. Inner loop: each coin. If coin fits, update dp of amount with the minimum of its current value and dp of amount minus coin plus 1. After both loops, if dp of total exceeds total, return negative 1. Otherwise return dp of total. Time: O of total times coins. Space: O of total.
